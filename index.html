Our story

Introduction Sweet Bakery

Sweet Bakery was born from a vision of helping others.

Sweet Bakery was born out of a passion of bringing healthy, all natural (many organic) whole food options to help others navigate food intolerances without feeling deprived because of a special diet and to do so in the safest way.Sweet Bakery has been providing safe products to the marketplace since 2020, both through retail and through wholesale (coffee shops, health food shops, and gourmet grocery stores). It’s also been important to us that our products contain only exceptional ingredients, using many organic and whole foods in our recipes. It sets us apart from other specialty bakeries. We are unapologetically proud of our choice to use only the best, even if our price point is more than our competitors. We only want the best experience from the first look at our products to the very last bite of our treats!
